Types of Architecture

Audiovisual architect

Audiovisual architects or engineers are specialists who focus on custom audio and visual entertainment systems.

Building architect

A building architect designs and plans out the elements of buildings. These professionals work in a variety of settings, including government sectors, public organizations and private firms.

Commercial architect

A commercial architect designs and develops ideas for commercial spaces. They may work for an architectural firm or a specific business that specializes in real estate management, for example.

Conservation/restoration architect

Conservation or restoration architects focus on repairing and restoring older buildings and other structures to resemble their original presentation.
